FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is TrackPro Logistics?

TrackPro Logistics is a logistics company that provides reliable and efficient logistics services, including air freight, road freight, sea freight, and warehouse storage.

How do I track my shipment?

You can track your shipment by using our real-time tracking system, which is available on our website and mobile app. Simply enter your tracking number and you will be able to see the latest updates on your shipment.

What is the delivery time for my shipment?

The delivery time for your shipment will depend on the service you have chosen and the destination of your shipment. You can check our website for estimated delivery times or contact our customer service team for more information.

Can I change my delivery address?

Yes, you can change your delivery address by contacting our customer service team. Please note that changes to your delivery address may affect the delivery time and cost of your shipment.

What happens if my shipment is lost or damaged?

If your shipment is lost or damaged, please contact our customer service team immediately. We will investigate the issue and work with you to resolve it as quickly as possible.
